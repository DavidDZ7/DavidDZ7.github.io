    Research Experience / Projects

    Here are some of my publications and projects:

    Ψ-NET: A Novel Encoder-Decoder Architecture for Animal Segmentation

    In this paper we propose a novel Ψ-Net architecture that consists of three encoders and a decoder for animal image segmentation. We created a new dataset were we manually labeled the ground-truth segmentation masks for 200 pig images. We trained our proposed model Ψ-Net on this dataset and compared the segmentation accuracy with the U-Net and Y-Net architectures. Our proposed model achieved the highest accuracy on the dataset with 93% pixel accuracy, and 81.6% mean intersection-over-union (IoU).
    • Skills: Python, Tensorflow, OpenCV, LaTex.
    See publication

    Impact of Pooling Methods on Image Quality Metrics

    In this paper we investigate the impact of different pooling techniques on the performance of image quality metrics. We have tested different pooling methods with the SSIM and S-CIELAB image quality metrics in the CID:IQ database, and found that the pooling technique has a significant impact on their performance.
    • Skills: MATLAB, LaTex.
    See publication

    Automatic Video Colorization with Deep Learning

    In this project I explored different encoder-decoder architectures for Image Colorization based on L*a*b* color space and created a framework for Automatic Video Colorization. I cleaned the places 365 dataset and trained 5 different models from scratch. I used VGG16 and ResNet50 for the transfer learning. Model M5 gave the best results with ResNet50 as backbone.
    • Skills: Python, Tensorflow, OpenCV, MATLAB.
    See project

    Domain Adaptation and Image Dehazing on Nighttime Hazy Image

    In this project, Milan, Thong and me, performed a domain adaptation module to dehaze nighttime hazy images. Milan developed a state-of-the-art GASDA algorithm to train on real and hazy images in order to obtain the depth of images, Thong implemented the CycleGAN to train domain adaption on our dataset (synthetic and real images) and I wrote the python scripts for web scraping publicly available real nighttime hazy images and implement different metrics in MATLAB for model evaluation such as MSE, PSNR, SSIM, CIEDE2000, BRISQUE, FADE. Our result outperformed existing state-of-the-art approaches in term of real nighttime image dehazing but the result for nighttime synthetic image dehazing was not good and needed for further research.
    • Skills: Python, MATLAB, Web Scrapping.
    See project

    Autonomous Heat Follower Vehicle.

    In this project, I used Python and a RaspberryPi3 for image processing of an Adafruit AMG8833 IR Thermal Camera. The heat image provided by the camera module is processed to determine the location of the heat source and control the direction of 2 dc motors. The user can see the heat image in real-time on his smartphone by using VNC viewer. It also uses an ultrasonic sensor HC-SR04 to measure distance and avoid obstacles. Moreover, I designed the vehicle's chassis in SolidWorks.
    • Skills: Python, RaspberryPi, Image Processing, SolidWorks, Mechanical Design.
